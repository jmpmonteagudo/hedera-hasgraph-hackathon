Your keys will be used to interact with ArtGraph's smart contracts. These keys will be used to identify you and your activities within the network.

Your public key is your identity, and may be shared with anyone. Think of this as your user name. Your private key, on the other hand is a secret, and may not be shared with anyone. Think of this as your password.

About

ArtGraph enables artists to be paid instantly, as their art is being experienced.

Imagine going to an art gallery where there's a QR code on each frame. When you scan it, you pay the artist with a micro transaction. Or, imagine clicking a link an listening to music paying for it, directly to the artist, as you listen.

Create your own originals, or remix from existing ones, and everyone gets paid - with no delays, no middlemen, and guaranteed by blazing fast smart contracts running on the Hedera Hashgraph platform.
